Teens Can Blog building younger generation with technology

At the programme, the Special Assistant to Governor of Abia State and teens coach, Mrs. Chinemenma Umeseka tasked the attendees on the need to build good netiquette. She emphasised the unexpected dangers behind wrong technological etiquette. She advised all attendees to keep learning and put everything they have learned to practice. The founder, Teens Can Blog, a Forbes-trained entrepreneur, Odoemena Chinonso, said: “There will be no job because the world is going digital and if you are not technologically wise, you will lack opportunities for the future. As a teenage blogger, you will spend a large amount of your time writing about topics you love and when you have an audience, the money will follow. “Teens Can Blog is focused on building the younger generation with high-tech activities,” he said.
